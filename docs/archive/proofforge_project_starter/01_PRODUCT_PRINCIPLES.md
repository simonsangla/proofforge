# Product Principles

## Positioning
ProofForge is a proof engine, not a gallery.

## Non-Negotiables
1. **Signal over volume**  
   Every published item must say something useful about capability, judgment, or execution quality.

2. **Consistency over novelty**  
   The system is optimized for repeatable weekly output, not random one-off experiments.

3. **Architecture before polish**  
   Information architecture, artifact structure, and storage discipline come before animation or visual embellishment.

4. **Public proof, private moat**  
   Show enough to convince. Do not ship the part that makes replacement easy.

5. **Fast publishing path**  
   Adding a new artifact must be low-friction and not require touching many files.

6. **Clean aesthetics, low noise**  
   Elegant, neutral, dark-or-warm visual direction. No blue-heavy SaaS look.

7. **Commercial readiness**  
   Every artifact should support eventual consulting, freelance, productized service, or premium template monetization.
